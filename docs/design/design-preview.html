Color — white, ink, and aubergine

Restrained: pure white paper, near-black ink, and ONE working color — a deep aubergine, regal and completely unowned in messaging. Serious without ever being an alarm. Error red stays clearly brighter and warmer, so the two never blur. The accent is a single swap-point token: change two hex values and the whole system (buttons, badges, verified marks, rosette ink family) follows.

The Rosette — guilloché identity, engraved from the key

Banknotes and passports carry fine-line guilloché engravings because they are mathematically hard to forge — the exact right heritage for a mark derived from a cryptographic fingerprint. Deterministic: petal count, waveform, phase, and ink are all read from the key's bytes. One curated 12-ink engraving palette, never random RGB. These are live — the algorithm runs in this page.

Scale + the verified engraving

Verified = a second concentric guilloché band engraved around the rosette, plus the quiet aubergine ✓ by the name. Never a shield, never a warning; unverified marks render at full confidence.
